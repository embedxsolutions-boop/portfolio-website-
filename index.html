Intelligent Water Management System

  • On-board LCD for local status display

PMC Robot

Breaker Status 2-Factor Authentication Robot

Engineered to prevent industrial accidents caused by SCADA misinterpretation, the PMC Robot physically visits and verifies breaker status with onboard cameras and sensors. Control room engineers can now remotely monitor actual breaker states in real-time from anywhere in the world, creating a critical safety layer between human decision-making and high-voltage equipment.

  • Physical 2FA for breaker status verification
  • Prevents accidents from SCADA misinterpretation
  • IoT enabled — globally controllable
  • Real-time live feed to control room engineers
  • Autonomous navigation to breaker panels

BVM System

Battery Voltage Monitoring for Forklifts

The BVM (Battery Voltage Monitor) is a precision IoT system designed for forklift battery management in industrial facilities. It measures per-cell voltages and calculates accurate State of Charge (SOC) in real-time, enabling warehouse and logistics operators to optimize battery health, prevent unexpected shutdowns, and extend battery lifespan significantly.

  • Per-cell voltage monitoring (Cell 1–4+)
  • Accurate SOC % calculation and display
  • Fully programmable & user-configurable thresholds
  • Custom mobile app with live dashboard
  • Prevents forklift downtime from battery failure

Smart Energy Meter Gateway

Secure Industrial Energy Data Acquisition System

Engineered specifically to address energy data security threats in SCADA environments, this gateway provides a completely isolated, cybersecure data acquisition pipeline. Operating over Modbus TCP protocol with fiber optic capability, it can transmit critical energy telemetry over 1,000+ kilometers, making it ideal for large-scale utility and industrial deployments.

  • Cybersecure isolated data acquisition pipeline
  • Modbus TCP protocol for industrial compatibility
  • Fiber optic transmission over 1,000+ km

  • Touchscreen TFT display with smart UI
